## 1. HAYS (Staffing — 280,000 plaatsingen/jaar, 12,800 consultants)

### Context
- 12,800 werknemers wereldwijd, 33 landen
- 280,000+ plaatsingen jaarlijks (77,000 permanent + 244,000 temporary)
- Marktleider in UK en Asia Pacific
- Doet workforce advisory, niet alleen recruitment

---

### HUIDIG (Product-feature stijl)

**Origineel:**
```
• Improve candidate satisfaction with seamless handoff from Service Cloud to 12,800 specialist consultants
• Route 280,000 annual placements via intent-level pairing beyond skills-based routing
• Scale personalized advisory using AI Supervisor for continuous triaging without experience degradation
```

**Verbeterd:**
```
• Improve candidate placement efficiency with seamless handoff from Service Cloud to 12,800 specialist consultants across 33 countries
• Route 280,000+ annual career inquiries via intent-level pairing based on career goals beyond keyword matching
• Scale workforce advisory services using AI triaging for candidate prioritization without consultant burnout
```

**Wat is veranderd en waarom:**

| Regel | Was | Is geworden | Waarom |
|-------|-----|-------------|--------|
| 1 | "Improve candidate satisfaction" | "Improve candidate placement efficiency" | "Satisfaction" is vaag; "efficiency" is meetbaar voor Hays |
| 1 | Geen locatie | "across 33 countries" | Context zegt: global presence in 33 countries |
| 2 | "placements" | "career inquiries" | "Placements" is het resultaat; "inquiries" is wat er binnenkomt |
| 2 | "beyond skills-based routing" | "beyond keyword matching" | Staffing gaat over CV/keyword matching, niet skills routing |
| 3 | "personalized advisory" | "workforce advisory services" | Hun eigen woorden uit context ("workforce solutions") |
| 3 | "AI Supervisor for continuous triaging" | "AI triaging for candidate prioritization" | Korter, actiegerichter |
| 3 | "without experience degradation" | "without consultant burnout" | Burnout is hun specifieke pijnpunt (hoge turnover staffing) |

---

### ERIC-STIJL (Actiegericht)

**Origineel:**
```
• Connect 12,800 specialist consultants directly with candidates without manual queue management
• Match placement inquiries automatically to the right recruiter based on intent, not just skills
• Handle 280,000 annual placements without adding headcount or losing service quality
```

**Verbeterd:**
```
• Connect job seekers directly to specialist recruiters without application black holes
• Match career inquiries automatically to available consultants based on job-change intent, not just CV keywords
• Place 280,000+ candidates annually without adding recruitment headcount or burning out your team
```

**Wat is veranderd en waarom:**

| Regel | Was | Is geworden | Waarom |
|-------|-----|-------------|--------|
| 1 | "12,800 specialist consultants" | "job seekers" | Focus op wie er geholpen wordt (candidates), niet de tools |
| 1 | "without manual queue management" | "without application black holes" | "Application black holes" is specifiek pijnpunt (candidates horen niets terug) |
| 2 | "placement inquiries" | "career inquiries" | Algemener - niet alleen voor directe plaatsing |
| 2 | "intent" | "job-change intent" | Concreter: wanneer is iemand ready om te switchen |
| 2 | "not just skills" | "not just CV keywords" | Staffing gaat over CV screening, niet skills databases |
| 3 | "Handle 280,000 annual placements" | "Place 280,000+ candidates annually" | Actief werkwoord "Place" ipv "Handle" |
| 3 | "losing service quality" | "burning out your team" | Burnout is relevanter dan abstracte "quality" |

## Totale Samenvatting per Stijl

### HUIDIG (Product-feature) — Kernverbeteringen

**Wat consequent is verbeterd:**
- "Satisfaction" → specifiekere outcome (delivery, efficiency, advisory experience)
- "AI Supervisor for continuous triaging" → "AI triaging/prioritization" (korter)
- Vaag "experience degradation" → specifiek pijnpunt (burnout, bottlenecks, expertise gaps)
- "Skills-based routing" → industry-specifieke matching (keyword, clearance, content type)

### ERIC (Actiegericht) — Kernverbeteringen

**Wat consequent is verbeterd:**
- Algemene "handoff delays" → specifieke bottleneck (application black holes, phone trees, AM bottlenecks)
- "Intent" → concrete triggers (job-change intent, life events, content type)
- "Without adding headcount" → specifiekere rol (recruitment headcount, project management teams)
- "Quality/experience" → meetbaar risico (burning out team, missing deadlines, compliance delays)
